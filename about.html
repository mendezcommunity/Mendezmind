🎯 What We Offer

Mendez Community is a digital platform that connects users with genuine sponsor engagement opportunities. Here's what you'll find on our platform:

  • Reward Zone (Silver Task): A curated section featuring verified sponsor offers. Users who engage with these offers earn reward points credited only after server-side verification of genuine engagement.
  • Honest Reward System: Reward points are credited only after the ad network verifies a genuine view via server-side postback. This is not a guaranteed salary — payouts are made from actual ad revenue after admin review.
  • Ad Safety Filter: A JavaScript-based MutationObserver filter that actively blocks known scam domains and fraudulent keywords in real time.
  • Community Transparency: We publish our ad quality guidelines, blocked domain lists, and update them publicly whenever new threats are identified.
  • PWA Support: Our platform works as a Progressive Web App — installable on your home screen for a native app-like experience.

✅ Reward points are real but not guaranteed income. Payouts depend on actual ad revenue and admin verification. We never promise fixed salaries or guaranteed cash.

📖 Our Story

Mendez Community was founded with a simple belief: the internet deserves better advertising. Too many reward platforms are built on fake promises, scam offers, and aggressive ad tactics that harm users.

We set out to build something different — a platform where transparency is non-negotiable, where every ad is reviewed before it reaches our users, and where the reward system is honest about what it is and what it isn't.

Since our launch, we have continuously improved our ad safety systems, removed fraudulent networks, and built a community of users who trust us to protect their experience. Our ad quality guidelines are publicly documented and updated regularly.

🚫 Our Ad Safety Commitment

We take ad safety seriously. Here is what we actively block and why:

  • Fake Reward Scams: Domains like phonepe-reward.com, paytm-cashback.net — blocked permanently.
  • Spin-to-Win Fraud: spin-win.in and similar "spin & win" scam sites — blocked.
  • Malware Distributors: calivexia.com, obviouslyintimatemink.digital — blocked after user reports.
  • Fake App Downloads: "iOS Launcher", "Luxury iPhone", "Transform Your Android" — keyword-blocked.
  • Dead/404 Ad Scripts: Monetag script (404 since July 2026) — removed entirely.

We use a real-time JavaScript MutationObserver filter on every page to catch and remove any ad that matches our blocklist — even if injected dynamically by an ad network.
